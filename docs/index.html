Design principles

🏠 Local-first

Your files stay on your disk. No cloud account, no sync service, no data leaving your machine. Everything works offline.

⚙ Config-driven

One TypeScript file defines your entire workspace. Tabs, groups, panels, templates — all in hub.config.ts. No UI wizards needed.

🤖 AI-augmented, not AI-dependent

Every feature works without AI. The AI gateway is optional and pluggable — use Claude, GPT, Ollama, or nothing at all.

🚀 Launchpad, not destination

The Hub indexes, orients, and dispatches. It sends you to the right tool with context — it doesn't try to replace your editor or wiki.
